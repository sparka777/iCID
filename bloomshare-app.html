🌾 Cornflowers (Centaurea cyanus)

Classic UK cutting flower - hardy & pollinator-friendly

🌱 When to Sow: Direct sow outdoors: April–June

💧 Where to Plant: Full sun. Space 15–20cm apart.

✂️ When to Harvest: Cut when buds are just starting to open

💐 Vase Life: Lasts 7–10 days

🌿 Permaculture Tip: Let some self-seed for next year's free flowers!

🐝 Companion Planting: Plant near beans - they fix nitrogen naturally

♻️ Native wildflower - Saved £8.50 vs shop-bought

🌸 Sweet Peas (Lathyrus odoratus)

Fragrant climbers perfect for British gardens

🌱 When to Sow: October (autumn) or March (spring)

💧 Where to Plant: Rich soil, full sun to partial shade

✂️ When to Harvest: Pick regularly for more blooms

💐 Vase Life: 5–7 days with daily water changes

🌿 Permaculture Tip: Nitrogen fixer - improves soil naturally!

💧 Water-Wise: Deep water weekly, mulch to retain moisture

♻️ Soil improver - Adds nitrogen naturally

🌺 Dahlias

Show-stopping blooms from summer to frost

🌱 When to Plant: Plant tubers April-May after frost

💧 Where to Plant: Full sun, rich well-drained soil

✂️ When to Harvest: Cut when fully open in morning

💐 Vase Life: 4–6 days, remove lower leaves

🌿 Permaculture Tip: Lift tubers in autumn, store for next year

🐞 Pest Control: Attract ladybirds with nearby fennel

♻️ Perennial investment - Tubers multiply yearly

🌼 Cosmos

Easy-care flowers that bloom all summer

🌱 When to Sow: April-May indoors, or direct sow May

💧 Where to Plant: Full sun, poor to average soil

✂️ When to Harvest: Cut when petals just open

💐 Vase Life: 5–7 days

🌿 Permaculture Tip: Thrives in poor soil - no feeding needed!

🌱 Seed Saving: Let dry on plant, store in paper bags

♻️ Self-sowing champion - Zero maintenance

🌻 Sunflowers

Giants that feed birds and brighten borders

🌱 When to Sow: April-May direct in ground

💧 Where to Plant: Full sun, any well-drained soil

✂️ When to Harvest: Cut when petals start to open

💐 Vase Life: 6–12 days in clean water

🌿 Permaculture Tip: Leave heads for birds in winter

🐝 Wildlife Value: Feeds bees, then finches love the seeds

♻️ Wildlife superstar - Feeds pollinators & birds

🔔 Foxgloves (Digitalis)

Woodland beauties loved by bumblebees

🌱 When to Sow: May-July for flowers next year

💧 Where to Plant: Partial shade, moist soil

✂️ When to Harvest: Cut when 1/3 of bells open

💐 Vase Life: 5–10 days

🌿 Permaculture Tip: Self-seeds readily in shady spots

⚠️ Safety: Toxic - wear gloves when handling

♻️ Bumblebee magnet - Essential for long-tongued bees
