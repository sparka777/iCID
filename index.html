🌾 Cornflowers (Centaurea cyanus)

Classic UK cutting flower - hardy & pollinator-friendly

🌱 When to Sow: Direct sow outdoors: April–June

💧 Where to Plant: Full sun. Space 15–20cm apart.

✂️ When to Harvest: Cut when buds are just starting to open

💐 Vase Life: Lasts 7–10 days

🌿 Permaculture Tip: Let some self-seed for next year's free flowers!

🐝 Companion Planting: Plant near beans - they fix nitrogen naturally

♻️ Native wildflower - Saved £8.50 vs shop-bought

🌸 Sweet Peas (Lathyrus odoratus)

Fragrant climbers perfect for British gardens

🌱 When to Sow: October (autumn) or March (spring)

💧 Where to Plant: Rich soil, full sun to partial shade

✂️ When to Harvest: Pick regularly for more blooms

💐 Vase Life: 5–7 days with daily water changes

🌿 Permaculture Tip: Nitrogen fixer - improves soil naturally!

💧 Water-Wise: Deep water weekly, mulch to retain moisture

♻️ Soil improver - Adds nitrogen naturally
